Okay, how many Linux users are there on Steam?
There's no ideal way to know this. We don't know the total number of users on Steam, sadly Valve rarely give that information out. However, we do know the current record for concurrent online users, which is: 18,528,722 from January 2018.

Using the data we have for January 2018, how many Linux users may have been online at that time?
Linux online total: 46,321
Linux online total without Simplified Chinese: 142,671
Linux online total just English: 290,900

To be clear, that's how many Linux users could have actually been online at that time.

As for an estimation of the total number of Linux users on Steam? Valve reported they had 125 million "active" Steam users back in 2015, considering Valve's rate of growth let's put that at 150 million active users now.
