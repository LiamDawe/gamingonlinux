To submit content to GamingOnLinux, you need to have setup your profile correctly. We need a real name, short bio and an author mugshot. Why do we require this? It's a case of accountability. Our readers want to know who is behind an article, so do we.

What you need to do:
  • Add an author picture/mugshot, the bottom field on the Avatar upload form.
  • Add a short description of yourself, at the bottom here.
  • If you're not using your real name, for now you need to email us to change it.

If you don't want to do all that, no hard feelings! Sending an email tip is probably better for you!
