[block: timeline]
A timeline of notable events for Linux gaming.

November 2012

Steam for Linux officially enters limited access beta.

December 2012

Steam for Linux beta access opened to all.

February 2013

Steam for Linux officially released.

June 2013

Steam adds support for 64bit Linux games.

November 2015

Steam Machines, Steam Link & Steam Controller officially released & SteamOS sale.

August 2018

Valve announce their new Steam Play "Proton", using a modified version of Wine to play Windows games on Linux.

[/block: timeline]
